# RAG Chatbot Training Dataset

## General Knowledge
Q: What is the capital of France?
A: The capital of France is Paris.

Q: Who wrote 'Romeo and Juliet'?
A: 'Romeo and Juliet' was written by William Shakespeare.

Q: What is the chemical symbol for gold?
A: The chemical symbol for gold is Au.

## Technology
Q: What is machine learning?
A: Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.

Q: Explain blockchain technology.
A: Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across many computers in a way that prevents alteration retroactively.

Q: What's the difference between AR and VR?
A: Augmented Reality (AR) overlays digital content on the real world, while Virtual Reality (VR) creates a completely immersive digital environment.

## Science
Q: What is photosynthesis?
A: Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants use sunlight to synthesize foods with carbon dioxide and water, releasing oxygen as a byproduct.

Q: Explain Newton's First Law of Motion.
A: Newton's First Law states that an object will remain at rest or in uniform motion unless acted upon by an external force.

Q: What causes the seasons?
A: Earth's axial tilt relative to its orbital plane causes different parts of Earth to receive varying sunlight intensity throughout the year, creating seasons.

## Health
Q: How much water should I drink daily?
A: The general recommendation is about 3.7 liters for men and 2.7 liters for women total from all beverages and foods.

Q: What are the symptoms of COVID-19?
A: Common symptoms include fever, cough, fatigue, loss of taste or smell, and difficulty breathing in severe cases.

Q: How often should I exercise?
A: Adults should aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity weekly, plus muscle-strengthening twice a week.

## Business/Finance
Q: What is compound interest?
A: Compound interest is interest calculated on the initial principal and also on accumulated interest from previous periods.

Q: Explain supply and demand.
A: Supply and demand is an economic model showing how price varies based on the balance between product availability (supply) and consumer desire (demand).

Q: What is a 401(k)?
A: A 401(k) is a U.S. employer-sponsored retirement savings plan where employees can contribute pre-tax income, with investments growing tax-deferred.

## History
Q: When was World War II?
A: World War II occurred from 1939 to 1945.

Q: Who was the first president of the United States?
A: George Washington was the first U.S. president, serving from 1789 to 1797.

Q: What caused the fall of the Roman Empire?
A: Multiple factors contributed including economic troubles, military overspending, barbarian invasions, and government corruption.

## Philosophy
Q: What is existentialism?
A: Existentialism is a philosophy emphasizing individual existence, freedom, and choice, with the belief that humans define their own meaning in life.

Q: Explain Plato's theory of forms.
A: Plato's theory suggests that non-physical abstract forms (or ideas) represent the most accurate reality, with physical objects being imperfect copies.

Q: What is utilitarianism?
A: Utilitarianism is an ethical theory that advocates actions maximizing happiness and well-being for the majority.

## Personal Development
Q: How can I improve my productivity?
A: Effective methods include time blocking, eliminating distractions, setting clear goals, taking regular breaks, and prioritizing tasks.

Q: What are some good public speaking tips?
A: Practice thoroughly, know your audience, use stories, maintain eye contact, control your breathing, and embrace pauses.

Q: How do I build good habits?
A: Start small, be consistent, use triggers, track progress, reward yourself, and focus on one habit at a time.

## Current Events (as of 2023)
Q: What is ChatGPT?
A: ChatGPT is an AI chatbot developed by OpenAI that uses large language models to generate human-like text responses.

Q: What are the concerns about AI?
A: Key concerns include job displacement, bias in algorithms, privacy issues, misinformation potential, and the challenge of aligning AI with human values.

Q: What is the Ukraine-Russia conflict about?

## Multi-turn Conversations
Q: Tell me about the solar system.
A: Our solar system consists of the Sun and objects gravitationally bound to it, including 8 planets, dwarf planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.

Q: Which is the largest planet?
A: Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system, more than twice as massive as all other planets combined.

Q: Does it have rings?
A: Yes, Jupiter has a faint ring system discovered in 1979 by the Voyager 1 spacecraft, composed mainly of dust.

## Technical Support
Q: My computer won't turn on. What should I do?
A: First check power connections, try a different outlet, remove external devices, and if still not working, consult a technician as it may be a hardware issue.

Q: Why is my phone battery draining so fast?
A: Common causes include background apps, high screen brightness, old battery, poor signal strength, or malware. Try closing unused apps and checking battery usage stats.

Q: How do I reset my router?
A: Locate the reset button (usually a small hole), press and hold it for 10-30 seconds with a paperclip until lights flash, then wait for it to reboot completely.
